Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Additional fees may apply.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase. Please bring your receipt and the item in its original condition.

Do you offer custom arrangements?

Yes, we specialize in custom floral arrangements. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for custom orders.
